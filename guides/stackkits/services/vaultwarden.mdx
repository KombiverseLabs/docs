Vaultwarden is the Bitwarden-compatible password vault exposed at `vault.<domain>` when enabled.

## Prerequisites

<Note>
  Vaultwarden has its own app login. Protect the route and configure app-level admin settings before adding real secrets.
</Note>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vaultwarden">
    Open `https://vault.<domain>` from the Node Hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the owner account">
    Register the initial account and store recovery information separately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock down signups">
    Disable open registration unless this is intentionally a private onboarding window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify

- The owner account can sign in.
- New account creation follows your intended policy.
- Backups cover the Vaultwarden data volume.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The vault opens but sync fails">
    Check that the public URL, TLS mode, and browser extension URL all match the same `vault.<domain>` route.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
