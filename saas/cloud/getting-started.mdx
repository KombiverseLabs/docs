Get started with kombify Cloud Cloud in 3 simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit [kombify.io](https://kombify.io) and create an account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select Free, Pro, or Enterprise tier
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch a tool">
    Click on KombiStack or KombiSim to launch with SSO
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plans

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------------|
| **KombiStack instances** | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Nodes per stack** | 3 | 20 | Unlimited |
| **KombiSim simulations** | 2 | 10 | Unlimited |
| **Team members** | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Support** | Community | Email | Priority |
