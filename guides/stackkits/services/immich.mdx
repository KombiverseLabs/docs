Immich is the photo and video management app exposed at `photos.<domain>` when enabled.

## Prerequisites

<Note>
  Plan storage before enabling mobile backups. Photos and videos can grow faster than other homelab data.
</Note>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Immich">
    Open `https://photos.<domain>` from the Node Hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the owner account">
    Complete first-run setup and confirm that the app URL matches `photos.<domain>`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect mobile backup">
    Install the Immich mobile app, sign in, and start with a small album or limited backup set.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify

- Uploads complete from a browser or mobile client.
- The storage path has enough free space.
- Background jobs are not stuck after the first import.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Mobile backup cannot connect">
    Confirm the URL, TLS mode, and whether the phone can resolve `photos.<domain>` on the current network.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
