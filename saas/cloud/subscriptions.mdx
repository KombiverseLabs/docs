# Subscriptions

Manage your KombiSphere subscription and billing.

## Plans

### Free
- 1 KombiStack instance
- 3 nodes per stack
- 2 KombiSim simulations
- Community support

### Pro ($29/month)
- 5 KombiStack instances
- 20 nodes per stack
- 10 KombiSim simulations
- Email support

### Enterprise (Custom)
- Unlimited instances
- Unlimited nodes
- Unlimited simulations
- Priority support
- Custom SLA

## Manage subscription

Access billing portal at Settings > Billing
