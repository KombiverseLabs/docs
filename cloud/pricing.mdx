## Billing

### Billing cycle

- **Monthly** — Pay each month, cancel anytime
- **Annual** — Pay yearly, get 2 months free (17% savings)

### Payment methods

- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only)

### Invoices

Access invoices from **Settings** → **Billing** → **Invoices**

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to billing">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Billing**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose plan">
    Select your desired plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review changes and confirm
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    New features available immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Prorated billing

When upgrading mid-cycle:
- Only pay the difference for remaining days
- New features activate immediately

## Downgrading

When downgrading:
- Changes take effect at end of billing cycle
- Excess homelabs/members become read-only
- Data is retained for 30 days after limits exceeded

<Warning>
If you exceed plan limits after downgrading, you won't be able to add new homelabs or members until you're within limits.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<Accordion title="What counts as a 'connected homelab'?">
A connected homelab is one stack connected via the kombify Agent. Each unique Agent connection counts as one homelab, regardless of how many nodes it contains.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
Yes! Upgrade anytime for immediate access to new features. Downgrade requests take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. During this time, you can reactivate and restore access. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for annual subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime but are not refunded for the current period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a discount for non-profits?">
Yes! Non-profit organizations receive 50% off all plans. Contact support with proof of non-profit status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I pay annually?">
Yes! Annual billing gives you 2 months free (17% savings). Switch to annual from **Settings** → **Billing**.
</Accordion>
