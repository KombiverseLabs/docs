Get kombify AI running and have your first conversation in minutes. Choose your deployment model:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SaaS (Recommended)">
    ## Using kombify Cloud

    kombify AI is built into kombify Cloud. No setup required if you have a Cloud account.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Log in to kombify Cloud" icon="right-to-bracket">
        Go to [app.kombify.io](https://app.kombify.io) and sign in with your account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the AI chat" icon="comment">
        Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner, or navigate to the AI section in the sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start chatting" icon="keyboard">
        Ask anything about your homelab setup:

        ```
        What services should I run for a family photo cloud?
        ```

        kombify AI understands your infrastructure context and will give tailored recommendations.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable Guard (optional)" icon="shield-check">
        Navigate to **AI Settings > Managers > Guard** and enable autonomous monitoring. Guard will start observing your infrastructure and reporting issues.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      AI features are available on all Cloud plans. Higher tiers include more monthly AI credits and access to premium models.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Self-Hosted (BYOK)">
    ## Bring Your Own Keys

    Run kombify AI on your infrastructure with your own API keys.

    ### Prerequisites

    - A running kombify TechStack instance
    - Docker and Docker Compose
    - At least one AI provider API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google AI)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Deploy kombify AI" icon="docker">
        Add the AI service to your Docker Compose setup:

        ```yaml docker-compose.yml
        services:
          kombify-ai:
            image: ghcr.io/kombify/ai:latest
            ports:
              - "5270:5270"
            environment:
              - KOMBIFY_AI_MODE=selfhosted
              - KOMBIFY_TECHSTACK_URL=http://techstack:5260
            volumes:
              - ai-data:/data
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure your API keys" icon="key">
        Open the AI portal at `http://your-server:5270` and navigate to **Settings > API Keys**.

        Add your provider keys:
        - **OpenAI** — For GPT models
        - **Anthropic** — For Claude models
        - **Google AI** — For Gemini models

        Keys are encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored locally.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start chatting" icon="comment">
        Navigate to the chat interface and ask your first question:

        ```
        Help me set up a reverse proxy with Traefik for my homelab
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      In self-hosted mode, you are responsible for API costs with your providers. kombify AI tracks usage and shows cost estimates in the dashboard.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
